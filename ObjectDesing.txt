EngineController:
      Read Speed and Override acceleration commands when vehicle reaches governed speed

SpeedLimiter:
	    Controlling the maximum speed of the vehicle based on a configurable threshold

ConfigurationManager:
	    Manage the programmable speed threshold securely via interfaces such as OBD-II, CAN,   or external tools.

DriverAlertSystem:
	    Provide real-time driver feedback (dashboard indicators, audible beeps) when the governed speed is reached.

EventLogger:
	    Log speed governor activations for fleet management and compliance audits.
